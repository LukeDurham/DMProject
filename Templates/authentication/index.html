What is DMProject?

We are a team of 4 SE students at MU. We have been given the task to help keep track of used vehicle inventory for local automotive dealership Dutchmiller.



The primary function of this website is for employees to be able to track inventory at their stores location as well as for upper levels of management to be able to access and find the vehicle from store to store.
